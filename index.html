OUR BEANS

Coffea arabica is a species of Coffee commonly cultivated in Nepal, the Mt. Everest Land.
It can be known as “Himalayan coffee” as well. Himalayan arabica coffee is the first species of coffee Arabica to be cultivated
in the Himalayan (mountain) range between the altitudes of 850m to 1950m above sea level in Nepal. Nepal is already famous
for its Himalayan Tea all over the world. And now it's turn for the Himalayan Coffee.
